Cidery

Overlook the beautiful Warwick Valley while sipping on your favorite home made cider. Kick back and relax on the great lawn in some lawn chairs and watch the sun set.

Row Boating

Row boat with some turtles and ducks and have a romantic time on the pond!

Concerts in the Park

Concerts in the park are held every Wednesday and Friday at 7pm. Bring a lawn chair, grab a glass of wine, sit back and relax to the nights genre!

Gardens

Take a walk through the botanical gardens with miles of gardens. Butterfly gardens, gazebos, bird watching areas and more, the perfect setting for a romantic time.
